## Practice Notes

- **Enthusiasm** expressed through: pitch range (wider ˩˧˥ = more), tempo (faster = more), volume (louder = more)
- **Apathy** (˧˨˨) shows as: narrow pitch, slow tempo, soft volume, dark vowels
- **Appropriate calibration** crucial: too much enthusiasm (˩˥˩) seems fake; too little (˧˨) seems rude
- Physical embodiment: enthusiasm lives in the body (posture, gesture, facial expression)
- Sustaining enthusiasm requires genuine interest (fake enthusiasm exhausts)
- Cultural differences: some cultures reward restraint, others value expressiveness
- Professional contexts often require moderate enthusiasm (˩˧)
- Depression/exhaustion affect capacity for enthusiasm
- Practice accessing enthusiasm when feeling flat
