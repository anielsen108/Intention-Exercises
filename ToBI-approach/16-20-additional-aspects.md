# Additional Specialized Aspects (16-20)

Five more specialized intention domains: Status Signals, Curiosity Modes, Boundary Setting, Humor Styles, and Motivational States.

---

# 16. STATUS SIGNALS

Exploring high-status and low-status vocal displays, status claiming, and status negotiation.

## High Status Displays (1-25)

### 1. "I'll decide."

**Entitled authority**
- ToBI: H* L+H* L-L%
- Stress: **I'LL** decide
- Pause: I'll decide.
- Downward pitch, claiming power.

**Confident leadership**
- ToBI: H* L+H* L-L%
- Stress: I'll de**CIDE**
- Pause: I'll decide.
- Assured, taking charge.

**Aggressive dominance**
- ToBI: H+L* L+H* L-L%
- Stress: **I'LL** decide!
- Pause: I'll decide!
- Forceful, brooking no dissent.

---

### 2. "Come here."

**Commanding**
- ToBI: H* L+H* L-L%
- Stress: **COME** here
- Pause: Come here.
- Expecting compliance, high status.

---

- Stress: If that's okay with you?
- Pause: If that's okay with you?
- Rising pitch, seeking permission.

**Apologetic uncertainty**
- ToBI: L* L* L* L* L* L* L-H%
- Stress: If that's okay with you
- Pause: If that's okay with you?

---

### 27. "Sorry to bother you."

**Excessive apology**

From genuine inquiry to hostile interrogation.

- ToBI: L* L* L+H* L+H* L-H%
- Stress: How does that **WORK**?
- Pause: How does that work?
- Authentic interest, no agenda.

**Fascinated inquiry**
- ToBI: L+H* L* L+H* L+H* L-H%
- Stress: **HOW** does that work?
- Pause: How does that work?
- Engaged, eager to learn.

---

## Rhetorical Questions (86-100)

## Integration Notes

These five additional aspects expand the intention toolkit:
- **Status**: Awareness of power displays
- **Curiosity**: Quality of questioning
- **Boundaries**: Protecting self/others
- **Humor**: Playfulness and perspective
- **Motivation**: Energy and purpose

Combined with previous aspects, practitioners now have 20 distinct lenses for intention work.
