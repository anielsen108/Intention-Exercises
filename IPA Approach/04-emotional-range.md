## Practice Notes

- **Emotional authenticity**: Connect to real feelings, don't just mimic pitch
- **Gradation sensitivity**: Notice the subtle pitch differences between intensity levels
- **Body involvement**: Let emotion inform your whole instrument, not just pitch
- **Context matters**: Same pitch contour can convey different emotions in different contexts
- **Layering**: Practice blending multiple emotions in complex exercises (81-100)
