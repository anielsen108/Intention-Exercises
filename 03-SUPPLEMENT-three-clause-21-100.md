## Advanced Practice Notes

Three-clause mastery requires:
- **Breath architecture**: Plan three zones of breath support
- **Pitch mapping**: Decide reset points vs. through-lines
- **Energy pacing**: Distribute intensity across three beats
- **Semantic coherence**: Ensure all three parts serve unified meaning
- **Prosodic variety**: Avoid repetitive patterns across clauses
- **Listening practice**: Record and identify each intention shift
- **Physical grounding**: Use body to anchor each clause shift
- **Contextual framing**: Imagine full scenario for authentic delivery

These exercises prepare you for:
- Extended dramatic monologues
- Complex professional presentations
- Nuanced interpersonal communication
- Sustained narrative storytelling
- Multi-layered emotional expression
